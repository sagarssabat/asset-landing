about us

This is an IT security-focused company who focuses on asset management. We have been created by and are oriented for security professionals.

  • Information or Cyber Security is not just about securing an organization. It is also focused on managing and reducing risk. We believe this starts with identification.
  • We know we’re not the first company to tackle this issue, however, we are creating FOR security practitioners more than for profits. We are a for-profit, yet we’re also contributing to the industry by offering the solution at far lower prices than other solutions. This will allow the SMB (small to medium-sized businesses) markets to have access to solutions that are both high quality and effective.
  • The identification of assets on a network is the starting point to everything else in security. We cannot protect that which we are not aware of. Unfortunately, we can, and often are, hurt by that which we are not aware of. This has led to many breaches. Our mission is to give a solution to companies to overcome this problem that is easy to use and with a low total cost of ownership so that small/understaffed security teams can use it and not have a dedicated person to maintain it.
